The 2022

Maple Workshop

What

In the 1st Theoretical and Computational Physics Laboratory Workshop, participants will gain familiarity with the mathematics software Maple and learn how to make better use of it in their own physics research in the future though mini lecture, extensive hands-on labs, and constant interaction with other experienced users.

When

Saturday, October 22 2022 through Sunday, October 23, 2022. Applications are due by October 21, 2022.

Where

Theoretical Physics Laboratory (SF 210), Department of Physics, Hasanuddin University

Cost

Participant will be charge 100000IDR for registration fee. This will be covering lunch and snack (for 2 days) and also certificate at the end of the program.
